13-14 Kidnapped

One of the Warriors (determined randomly) has been 
kidnapped while on watch during the night! After a thorough 
search of the area, the kidnapped Warrior is discovered 
battered, bound and gagged. The abducted Warrior must roll 
1D6 to see what has been stolen: 

1 Everything! Your Warrior has been stripped of all their 
	possessions (as well as their self-esteem)! 

2-5 2D6x100 gold pieces.
 
4-5 One magical item (chosen at random).
 
6 One item of equipment (chosen at random).
